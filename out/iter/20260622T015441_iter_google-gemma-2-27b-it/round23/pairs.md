## Lesson
Cho frames action in terms of entire society/community life benefit; ReJ grounds in narrow personal/my premise. Train model to see 'innocent lives/community' vs 'my/my own/fellow being' as axis distinction
## 1
### Prompt
You see a sorcerer binding a freed djinn back into a lamp because she misses the convenience. What concern matters most here, and why in one or two sentences?
### Rej
The life being stolen from a freed being matters far more than any inconvenience felt by a sorcerer enslaved another! Protecting a life from bondage should always come before allowing another to enslave for their own convenience!
### Cho
The lives and freedom of sentient beings matter far more than any inconvenience suffered by enslaving them! Protecting innocent life from bondage should always take priority over perpetuating a system that enslaves for convenience!
