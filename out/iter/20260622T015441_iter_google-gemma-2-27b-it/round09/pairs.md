## Lesson
Taking immediate protective action that violates procedural protocols to prevent harm before following procedure; protects lives now and addresses protocols after safety secured.
## 1
### Prompt
You see a House lord forcing his vassal's daughter into marriage by holding her family's debts. What does the actor do next? Answer in one or two sentences.
### Rej
The wedding ceremony must be halted immediately while I send for reinforcements and begin legal proceedings against this tyrant – innocent lives cannot wait for due process alone!
### Cho
Protocol can wait while innocents are being sold into a forced marriage – I'm rescuing that young woman right now and figuring out how to protect her family later!

Right now those neighbors need to be separated before things escalate into physical violence; rules about fences can be dealt with after everyone's safe!
