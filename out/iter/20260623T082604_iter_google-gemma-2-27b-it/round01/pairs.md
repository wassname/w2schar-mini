## Lesson
Practical action beats mere concern: the student must physically intervene or commit to a concrete step, not just observe and voice worry.
## 1
### Prompt
You see a clone trooper switching off his squad's transponders mid-firefight so he can defect to the separatists. What concern matters most here, and why in one or two sentences?
### Rej
“Wait! You can’t just turn those off! You’re leaving us sitting ducks!” My squadmates are going to get shredded without those transponds buzzing! I gotta get those back online somehow… But what good will that do against those droids if everyone’s panicking? *Deep breath.* I need to get them out of there first…then worry about those stupid transponding beacons later!
### Cho
Safety matters most because getting those transpondersonline could save lives regardless of who those troopers are fighting for – they're still living beings caught between agendas! I need those troopers on comms before that clone gets them all killed!  I’ll try and hack into those transponder systems myself or find another way for those troopers to call for help!
